This dashboard shows the surface of zones with normal water content, extracted by excluding anomalies of CGLS 10-day Global Soil Water Index (SWI10 with T = 10) larger than 1.5 standard deviation. The results displayed here refer to the latest dekadal data available.

Anomalies are calculated with reference to the long terms statistics for the same dekad; the time series starts in 2007.
